Walk

Discover inspiring light artworks against the backdrop of winterly Amsterdam, by foot. The route is around 6.5 km.

THE FESTIVAL APP

With the festival you get everything out of your walking experience! The app contains relevant updates and information about the festival. In the app you can purchase several walking experiences:

LIGHT WALK

Walk along the festival route and listen to the audio guide, which tells you all about the artworks and festival theme Legacy. Or read all about the creative minds and processes behind the art. Turn on your location services and see exactly where you're walking. The Light Walk also contains exclusive offers at restaurants along the route.

AVAILABLE 27 NOV € 6.99

KIDS WALK

The Kids Walk is for little adventurers. It contains fun facts about the artworks, quiz questions and a scavenger hunt with a little prize. You can choose to walk the entire, or only half of the route. We suggest the Kids Walk for children between 7-12 years old, but younger is possible with guidance by a parent/caregiver.

AVAILABLE 27 NOV € 3.99

FOODWALK

Elevate your Light Walk in a culinary way with the Foodwalk! On the way, you will collect take-away bites and drinks at participating restaurants. There are six different Foodwalks this year:
